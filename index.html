Introduction:
Background of TeN

Digital Signage has proven to be an excellent addition to the mediums of communication to McGill students.

TeN can be found in 7 locations in the Engineering Complex, in three residences, in the Shatner Building, and in the Rutherford Physics Building.

Introduction:
Current TeN features

  • Location-specific slide deployment
  • Slide Override (Exam Schedule & Location)
  • Full-fleged administrator control panel
  • Remote root access to clients

Introduction:

Shi Zhuo

Hardware:
Current Hardware

  • TeN currently runs on a full-size Desktop PC
  • Costs $200-$300/ea
  • High power consumption ~ 100W
  • More than sufficient
  • Big and heavy to mount

Hardware:
Main Requirements

  • Ability to run HTML5, Javascript and CSS3
  • Required peripherals are HDMI, Ethernet/Wifi, USB
  • Reliable 24/7 service
  • Minimal cost and size
